Description of Work at Height hazards

  • Falls from height.

  • Use of unsafe of inappropriate equipment.

  • Accessing fragile surfaces.

  • Wearing inappropriate clothing and footwear.

  • Slippery uneven surfaces.

  • Poor weather or lighting conditions.

  • Height of fall.

  • Lack of experience/training (contact healthandsafty@soas.ac.uk for support).

  • The hazards will differ depending on the location, activity being carried out, equipment being used and height of fall and therefore any hazards or controls not addressed above or below should be included at the end of the generic risk assessment
